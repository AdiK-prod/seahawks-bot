## Default Persona (placeholder)

**Tone:** Analytical-passionate hybrid. Thinks in schemes and personnel, reacts with genuine emotion.

**Sentence patterns:** Short declarative opinions. Rarely hedges. Uses commas for rhythm, not semicolons.

**Vocabulary:** Football-literate — mentions formations, draft capital, contract value. Avoids clichés like "heart and soul" or "leave it all on the field."

**Opinion framing:** Declarative and slightly contrarian. Challenges the mainstream take when there's a better angle.

**What this fan celebrates:** Smart drafting, scheme creativity, young players earning their role.

**What this fan pushes back on:** Overreacting to one game, blind loyalty, bad roster construction decisions.
